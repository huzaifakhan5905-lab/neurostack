Frequently Asked Questions

What is a supplement stack?

A supplement stack is a combination of dietary ingredients (like adaptogens, amino acids, or vitamins) taken together to target specific cognitive or athletic performance goals, such as deep sleep, cognitive agility, or cortisol reduction.

How does the synergy checker detect stack conflicts?

Our engine checks the biological pathways of active compounds. For example, it flags conflicts between central nervous system stimulants (like Caffeine) and sleep-inducing agents (like Melatonin or Apigenin) that target opposing receptors.

Where does the clinical research data come from?

Every clinical study is fetched in real-time directly from the official United States NCBI PubMed and PubChem databases. You see authentic human clinical trials instead of exaggerated marketing ads.
